The Process

PURPOSE

No project is too big or too small—what matters most is purpose. Every film we create has a reason for being, and defining that reason becomes the foundation of our work. Once the purpose is clear, it serves as our roadmap, guiding us through each step of the process, from concept to completion.

CREATIVE

Creative collaboration is where the magic happens. We believe in working closely with our clients to bring fresh, authentic ideas to life. Together, we'll craft a vision that aligns with your brand's story, ensuring every decision feels true to your message and vision.

PRODUCTION

The fun part. With a solid pre-production plan in hand, we bring the vision to life. On set, we focus on execution with professionalism, positivity, and precision. No egos. No drama. Just top-tier talent, high-quality equipment, and a team that delivers results.

POST PRODUCTION

Once the footage is in hand, we work our magic in post-production. We polish every detail—editing, sound design, voiceover, VFX, and color grading—to ensure your final cut not only meets but exceeds expectations. Every frame, perfectly crafted.

About

At IRIDIUM FILMS, we are an all-encompassing film production company based in Salt Lake City, Utah. From commercials to documentaries, we specialize in bringing a wide range of projects to life. Our goal is simple: to produce visually striking films that create lasting impressions.
